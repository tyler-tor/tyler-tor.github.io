About Me

Instapostpix

Instapostpix is a full stack web application which allows user to interact with each other via posts and comments. Users can further their experience by adding friends and liking their posts. Sharing their experiences and keeping in touch with one another.

GitHub Live Site

Aktiv

Aktiv is a full stack web application that allows a user to track a route they may like to take for a bike ride or a walk/run. Also able create workouts notating the duration and type of activity. They can take this experience further by interating with other users and their activity, exploring their routes and workouts if they so choose.
